Ayoola Brimmo

Ayoola is an Engineer and social entrepreneur. He co-founded Cycled Technologies in 2016 to deliver waste management products and services that help buildings and municipalities recycle more and reduce logistics expenses. Since then, he has led operations at the company and championed the development of the Cycled Self Sorting SmartBin Technology which earned him an “MIT Technology Reviews Innovator Under 35” recognition. Ayoola also holds a PhD in Mechanical Engineering and by virtue of his scientific exploits, he was involved in the development of the Non-contact Multiphysics Probe, which is a groundbreaking addition to the toolkit of the Human Atlas Project -- the world's most ambitious genetic research project. Prior to co-founding Cycled Technologies, Ayoola served as the Executive Director of Nordoest which was the Middle East’s Representative of Innovation Norway — the Norwegian Government’s most important instrument for innovation and development of Norwegian enterprises. He also served as the Technical Director of Nordic Innovation Hub — a cleantech incubator in Masdar City Abu Dhabi, set up as a collaboration between the five Nordic countries and supported by Nordic Innovation. Ayoola will be speaking in the founder panel about passion and mission building through his venture.

Joel Alexander

Joel Alexander is the Co-Founder & CEO of CrowdPad, formerly working where they're building the next wave of social media by ensuring that creators are able to monetize since day 1, immune to deplatforming and demonetization plays by big-tech platforms.

CrowdPad recently raised $2.5M backed by Balaji S, IVC, Ali Abdaal, FTX Ventures (Serum Ecosystem Fund) & others, was featured as one of Sifted's 13 European Web3 Startups to Watch, and was also named a top creator economy company by both Antler & SpeedInvest (not investors in CrowdPad).

His background includes working for several top European startups in the creator economy & edtech categories, he is also a bit of a YouTuber himself having hustled his way into VC & Tech by doing podcasts with YC-backed founders.

Joel will be speaking in the founder panel about passion and mission building through his venture.

Dr. Barbara Scheck

Barbara Scheck is Professor of Entrepreneurship at Munich Business School / Germany and Visiting Faculty at NYUAD. She is the Co-Founder of several startups in the impact space and focuses on the financing of social enterprises, impact and mission-related investing, and impact assessment in her research.

Other significant experiences include consulting for a social venture capital fund and projects for development cooperation agencies on social entrepreneurship. This, together with the time she has spent on startup workshopping, leads her to have tangible awareness of startup roadmapping and key building blocks.

Barbara will be leading workshops in NYUAD Slush'D.

Simran Chowdhry

Simran is the Co-founder of BluePhin Technologies, Co-founder of Airtap, a startup Advisor, a UNGC Youth Ambassador, and a Finance graduate from the American University of Sharjah. She is a passionate, self-taught techie and social entrepreneur whose mission is to save the planet by decarbonizing our economy and pave the way for green innovation.

Simran has been featured on Entrepreneur ME, CNN, Dubai Eye, and several other sustainability podcasts for her unique journey building innovative solutions for environmental conservation. After doing several startup weekends and continuously taking part in every competition that came her way, she has since won the Sharjah EF Pitch Competition, Unilever's Young Entrepreneur Award, exhibited at GMIS, Gitex Future Stars, and others.

Simran will be leading several workshops in Slush'D x Violet Ventures and will be speaking about her experiences as a young founder in the UAE.

Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta

Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta has extensive knowledge in the entrepreneurship and investment ecosystems in EMEA, having served as the Head of Market Infrastructure, Digital and VC/FinTech for South Asia at ADGM, and as strategic advisors for various Fintech initiatives, amongst many other roles. At ADGM, he was responsible for developing the UAE ecosystem, which saw a substantial increase in Digital Banking, Venture Capital, FinTech, and Sustainable Finance companies listed in ADGM.

Amongst the companies he serves as a Strategic Advisor for include Peregrine Digital (decentralized investment platform), RuDo Wealth (personal finance management), Masary Capital (MENA’s first Emirati owned blockchain and crypto joint venture platform), Saffe (facial recognition technology), and Sheikh Hamdan Bin Ahmad Al Makhtoum’s Private Office.

Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta will be leading the session on the MENA ecosystem together with Devika Raveendrean and will be speaking about the UAE economy, common sectors, key customers, fundraising, and startup environments.

Devika Raveendran

Devika Raveendran is the Founder and CEO of DR Partners- a boutique corporate and wealth structuring advisory practice headquartered in Dubai with presence in Abu Dhabi and Delhi. As a Private Clients lawyer with diverse experience working for global trust companies and multifamily offices, she has been actively advising family enterprises in structuring their personal and business wealth using multi-generational & jurisdictional legacy structures, engaging with members for implementing family governance, coaching the rising generation for leadership & business succession along with crafting residency & relocation plans for ultra-high net worth families.

Devika is a cross border structuring expert advising families and corporates on consolidation of complex business ownership and restructuring for externalization of ownership to create tax optimized businesses. As a strategic advisor, she has been leveraging her 15+ years of experience in legal and corporate advisory to help early-stage companies & founders optimize their market entry in the region & facilitate access to capital from her well-established network of investors.

Amongst the several entities where she serves as an Advisory board Member, Sterve is the first-ever AI-powered integrated platform in the Middle East & Africa region for MSMEs to manage their commerce and finance through one app. The other notable startup is Mirathi that uses innovative technology for safekeeping of cryptos and seamless transfer of digital legacy.

Devika will be hosting the session on the MENA ecosystem together with Dr. Bhaskar Dasgupta and will be speaking about an overview of key legal considerations for business owners in the UAE, ranging from licensing to residency.

Aman Merchant

Aman Merchant is a mission-driven serial parallel entrepreneur, futurist, investor & advisor with 25+ years of experience across 12 ventures in education, social impact, and disruptive innovation. Aman is the Chief Provocateur at Radicle, passionate about transforming industries, cities, and lives through ideating and launching ambitious platforms for solving the wicked system problems that challenge humanity today.

While co-curating RewirEd (global education summit) for Dubai Cares, he launched the MENAP region’s first Edtech collective (ReimaginED Collective), having previously managed a Global Grand Challenges program for Expo2020 & a disruptive innovation program for Dubai Airport Free Zone, curated Abundance 360 Dubai (CEO Summit with Singularity University) with Dr. Peter Diamandis, launched Epiq360 (regional e-commerce & coaching platform for wellness/longevity/peak performance), and co-founded & led Impact Hub Dubai (global entrepreneurial network for positive impact).

Aman is a member of both the Young Presidents Organisation (YPO) & Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO), he holds a 1st Class Masters (Hons) Degree from Oxford University & is also a Stanford Business School & London School of Economics alum. An Entrepreneur Mentor in Residence at London Business School since 2014, he is the MENA region’s 1st Impact Investing Fellow at AVPN, UAE’s 1st Fellow with OnDeck Edtech, as well as a certified Peak Performance coach from the Flow Genome Project. He serves on the Global Council on SDG4 and was part of the investment committee at a $100m global impact fund (ExpoLive). Flow, biohacking, venture creation, system thinking, moonshots, global education systems & edtech are some of his research and teaching interests which he is pursuing at NYUAD.

At Slush’D, Aman will be hosting workshops and sharing insights stemming from his background and passion for societal and corporate problem-solving, innovation, and technology.

Peter Vesterbacka

Peter Vesterbacka is an Entrepreneur, Angel Investor, and former Mighty Eagle of Angry Birds (Rovio). With over 20 years of experience, Peter has co-created and built communities including the HP Bazaar, Mobile Monday, Slush, and Startup Sauna, to name a few. He served as the Mighty Eagle at Rovio Entertainment for many years, taking the brand to unprecedented heights and making Angry Birds a worldwide phenomenon.

Peter is currently an adjunct professor of Innovation and Entrepreneurship at Tongji University, one of the leading universities in China. In 2011, TIME Magazine named Peter one of the 100 most influential people on the planet. Today, he is extending a $15B underwater tunnel between Helsinki and Tallinn at FinEst Bay Area.

Elliott Denham

Elliott Denham sits at the intersection between business growth, people, and technology. Sometime in Q4 2018, he had an epiphany that he wasn't truly living, but merely existing. Since then, he mustered up the courage to volunteer his way around the world and give back, which has been an awesome experience.
Utmost joy to lead on the inception, development, and deployment of SANDBOX; the first-of-its-kind 12-month Startup Program for MVP-to-Seed stage tech entrepreneurs in UAE (sandbox.dtec.ae) Providing early-stage VC (seed & growth) Championing entrepreneurship across the GCC, especially aligned to the #Dubai2040 vision

Shamim Chowdhury

Shamim Chowdhury is an entrepreneur, Personal Growth Coach, Storytelling expert, and Advisor to many startups, companies, and government institutions. He has personally impacted and supported over 100 startups over a 3-year period, as well as played various corporate roles within Financial Technology, Programming, Testing, Support, Pre-Sales, Consulting, and Product Managing.

For the past 4 years, he has been obsessed with the 95% failure rate in startups. His mission is to empower many entrepreneurs like yourself to build businesses that are sustainable, scalable, and impactful. He pursues this mission by helping individuals develop an entrepreneurial mindset, grow their personal brands as founders, and coaching them to become effective storytellers.

Mohamed Amine Belarbi

Mohamed Amine Belarbi is the Founder and CEO of Project Cypher, one of the region's fastest-growing cybersecurity companies. The company helps tech giants better secure their platforms and data, while also assisting various marquee brands in the Middle East and North Africa in defending against cyber attacks and breaches. Project Cypher serves clients in the USA, North Africa, and the GCC, with a focus on R&D and product development, including their flagship product Cypherleak.

Mohamed launched Cypherleak.com in January of 2022 after 8 months of R&D and is currently scaling the B2B SaaS platform globally, with over 2200 corporate customers to date. In addition to his work in cybersecurity, Mohamed co-founded Halahi.com, a platform that allows fans to pay and request personalized video shoutouts and messages from their favorite celebrities. The platform currently lists hundreds of global celebrities and influencers with almost 400 million combined audience.

Mohamed has been recognized as "Young Entrepreneur of the Year" by Arabian Business, "10 top African change leaders" by African Leadership University, and was the youngest entrepreneur to ever be featured on the cover of Forbes Middle East Magazine.

Marc Mekki

Marc Mekki is a speaker, author, and coach specializing in innovation, design thinking, digital transformation, and agile leadership. With 20 years of experience taking up entrepreneurship challenges in China, Europe, and the Middle East, his mission is to demystify digital innovation and empower clients with the tools, insights, and skills to thrive in the digital age.

Marc is a gifted storyteller with an aptitude for explaining complex concepts in simple terms. His sessions expertly thread the fine line between immersive knowledge delivery and lighthearted, relatable engagement. He demystifies technology and cuts through the hype, leaving audiences with the exact knowledge and insights they need.

As a coach, Marc focuses on transformation, guiding high-potentials and seasoned executives alike to new levels of self-awareness, pragmatism, and knowledge. His greatest strength is helping individuals unlock pent-up creativity and strengthen their capacity for innovation.

Idriss Al Rifai

Idriss Al Rifai is an entrepreneur passionate about disrupting the Middle East retail market with technologies and customer-centric services that enable the growth of e-commerce in the region. He has developed and optimized a last-mile delivery solution specifically for the e-commerce needs and requirements in the Middle East.

In 2012, Idriss founded Fetchr, which has evolved to become a full end-to-end logistics solution, backed by technology, for e-commerce companies, regional players, social media sellers, as well as SMBs in the MENA region. Fetchr is also the first company in MENA to raise $52M in funding to date ($11M Series A and $41M Series B), led by a top-tier Silicon Valley VC, New Enterprise Associates.

Idriss's goal is to empower all deliveries through technology in emerging markets. Previously, he worked for MarkaVIP, a leading e-commerce player in the MENA region, where he headed the operations and built an in-house logistics department to expedite deliveries. Prior to this, he worked as a consultant at Boston Consulting Group (BCG). Idriss holds an MBA from the University of Chicago's Booth School of Business. He is also a Rath and Fulbright scholar and Endeavor entrepreneur.

Latifa Bin Haider

Latifa Bin Haider is an Emirati social entrepreneur. She earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the American University of Sharjah, where she majored in Management and minored in Finance. During her time at university, she co-founded Mental Health AE, an initiative aimed at raising awareness about the importance of mental health and providing a platform for those who haven't had the opportunity to voice their challenges.

Latifa's achievements include being selected as one of the top 10 teams to participate in the Ma'an Social Incubator - Abu Dhabi Government, as well as winning the Emirati Development Program with Baytuki. She also won two other entrepreneurship competitions with both Baytuki and Mental Health AE in StartAD (NYUAD).

As a young Emirati woman, Latifa is dedicated to empowering women in her society with opportunities and education. She brings fresh insight and understanding of the value of youth for the UAE's future to Baytuki. Latifa's leadership and inspiration are reflected in every item in her wardrobe, which she delicately picks out.
