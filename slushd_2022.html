SLUSH'D 2023

To Be Inspired.

NYUAD Slush’D 2023, the UAE’s largest student startup conference, marked a transformative milestone under the theme Million Dollar Mistakes. With a quadrupled speaker lineup, double the stages, and representation from 12 universities across the UAE, the event achieved a 2.8x increase in turnout compared to 2022, drawing 262 in-person attendees. Garnering over 44,600 digital impressions, it commanded regional attention and amplified its impact across the entrepreneurial ecosystem. Attendees immersed themselves in dynamic workshops, mentorship sessions, and vibrant networking opportunities, with 93% feeling more inspired to launch their own ventures. This conference was more than an event—it was a catalyst for innovation, equipping participants to transform setbacks into breakthroughs and building resilience for a brighter future. As the momentum continues to grow, NYUAD Slush’D looks ahead to 2025, promising even greater impact, innovation, and inspiration to redefine the entrepreneurial landscape in MENA.
