From Young Pioneering Founders to Experts on the UAE Ecosystem.

Peter Vesterbacka

Entrepreneur, Angel Investor, and former Mighty Eagle of Angry Birds (Rovio). Peter has over 20 years of experience in co-creating and building communities including the HP Bazaar, Mobile Monday, Slush, and Startup Sauna to name a few. He was the Mighty Eagle at Rovio Entertainment for many years taking the brand to unprecedented heights and making Angry Birds a worldwide phenomenon. Peter is also currently an adjunct professor of Innovation and Entrepreneurship at Tongji University, one of the leading universities in China. In 2011, TIME Magazine named Peter one of the 100 most influential people on the planet. Today, he is extending a $15B underwater tunnel between Helsinki and Tallinn at FinEst Bay Area.

Elliott Denham

I sit on the intersection between business growth, people and technology. Sometime in Q4 2018, I was going about my day - work, meetings, emails (maybe you can relate?) - then out of the blue, realized that I wasn't "living." I was merely "existing." Since then, I mustered up the courage to volunteer my way around the world; paying my dues by giving back. It's been awesome.

> Utmost joy to lead on the inception, development and deployment of SANDBOX; the first-of-its-kind 12-month Startup Program for MVP-to-Seed stage tech entrepreneurs in UAE (sandbox.dtec.ae)

> Providing early-stage VC (seed & growth)

> Championing entrepreneurship across the GCC, especially aligned to the #Dubai2040 vision

Shamim Chowdhury

Shamim is an entrepreneur, Personal Growth Coach, Storytelling expert, and Advisor to many startups, companies, and government institutions. He has personally impacted and supported over 100 startups over a 3-year period as well as played various corporate roles within Financial Technology, Programming, Testing, Support, Pre-Sales, Consulting, and Product Managing.

For the past 4 years, he was obsessed with the 95% Failure rate in Startups. His mission is to empower many entrepreneurs like yourself to build businesses that are sustainable, scalable, and impactful which he pursues through building an entrepreneurial mindset, growing personal brands of founders, and coaching them to become effective storytellers.

Mohamed Amine Belarbi

Mohamed is the Founder and CEO of Project Cypher, one of the region’s fastest growing cybersecurity companies helping tech giants better secure their platforms and data, as well as assisting various marquee brands in the Middle East and North Africa better defend against cyber attacks and cyber breaches. Project Cypher services clients in the USA, North Africa and the GCC, and has a focus on R&D and product development with a flagship product Cypherleak.

Mohamed launched Cypherleak.com in January of 2022 after 8 months of R&D, and is currently scaling the B2B SaaS platform globally with over 2200 corporate customers to date.

Mohamed co-founded Halahi.com, a platform that allows fans to pay and request personalised video shoutouts and messages from their favorite celebrities. The platform currently lists hundreds of global celebrities and influencers with almost 400 Million combined audience.

Mohamed has been recognised "Young Entrepreneur of the Year" by Arabian Business, "10 top African change leaders" by African Leadership University, and was the youngest entrepreneur to ever be featured on the cover of Forbes Middle East Magazine.
