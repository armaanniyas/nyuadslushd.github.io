The 2025 Hackathon

AI FOR GOOD.

The countdown is on! Ready to change the world with your ideas? This is your chance to make an impact. The AI for Good Hackathon, powered by NOKIA, the Platinum Sponsor of NYUAD Slush'D 2025, invites you to push boundaries, solve real-world challenges, and show the world what you’re capable of.

Registration Deadline: January 26, 2025, at 11:59 PM GST
Team Size: Up to 5 members (or sign up solo, and we’ll match you with a team!)
Prizes: First place will recieve 10,000 AED sponsored by ADCCI. Winners will receive exclusive prizes, a tour of Nokia HQ, and an opportunity to meet senior executives
What’s Needed: A passion for innovation and a drive to make a difference. This hackathon is designed for students from all backgrounds. Bring your creativity, collaboration skills, and ambition—we’ll provide the mentorship and tools to make your ideas shine.

The Timeline: Round 1
Jan 31—Feb 2: A 3-day online hackathon. Build your ideas from the comfort of your home while receiving mentorship and resources.
Round 2
Feb 8: The top 8 teams are invited to NYUAD Slush'D for a live pitch battle in front of an audience of tech leaders and visionaries from Nokia, startAD, and more!
