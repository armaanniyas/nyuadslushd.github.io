AS A STUDENT

Feast on Inspiring Insights:
Immerse yourself in captivating talks from industry pioneers who are shaping the future of AI in MENA. From navigating unexpected challenges to achieving groundbreaking success, these thought leaders will share their most valuable lessons, leaving you inspired and equipped to innovate boldly. Their stories will ignite your imagination and empower you to tackle challenges head-on.

Supercharge with Hands-On Workshops:
Step into dynamic workshops tailored to fuel your creativity and sharpen your skills. Learn to refine your pitch, analyze market opportunities, and craft compelling narratives for your ideas. These action-packed sessions, led by experts, will push your problem-solving abilities to the next level and prepare you to excel in any entrepreneurial or technical challenge.

Unparalleled Networking Opportunities:
Connect with AI visionaries, seasoned entrepreneurs, and investors eager to support transformative ideas. This is your chance to forge meaningful connections, gain mentorship, and uncover collaborations that could accelerate your journey. Who knows? Your next conversation could lead to your biggest breakthrough!

FOR PARTNERS

Connect with Visionary Minds:
Engage with a vibrant community of trailblazing entrepreneurs and innovators who are redefining the rules of the startup game. Exchange ideas, forge transformative partnerships, and collaborate with fearless changemakers determined to shape the future of AI and entrepreneurship in MENA.

Align Your Brand with Innovation and Impact:
NYUAD Slush'D is the ultimate platform to showcase your brand's commitment to innovation, sustainability, and youth empowerment. Gain unparalleled visibility through our digital platforms and event highlights, positioning your brand at the forefront of MENA’s entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Discover the Game-Changers of Tomorrow:
Be the first to identify groundbreaking ideas and untapped potential. Build strategic investments in startups led by bold visionaries ready to disrupt industries and transform the world. Partner with the next wave of innovators and leave a lasting mark on the AI-driven future of MENA.
